Why is a Video is not loading?

This might be due to either bad network or too much traffic on the website. Try connecting to a better internet provider and try again.

Why isn't there a HD version of this video?

This is a website built for THE PROTON GUY's portfolio. In order to host hd quality videos, it requires resources He does not currently have. Look at the bright side, the current video quality isn't too bad :)

Why is the sound distorted?

That might be you phone's speaker :)

Why is the Video stuttering, buffering or randomly stopping?

This is probably due to bad network. Connect to a better internet provider and try again.

How do I make the Video go Fullscreen?

Just at the bottom right of the video player, theres an incon that signifies screen expansion. When clicked, it opens fullscreen mode.
